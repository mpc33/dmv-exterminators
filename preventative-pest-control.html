Proactive Pest Protection

Don't wait for a pest problem to arise. Our preventative pest control services offer regular inspections, barrier treatments, and exclusion services to keep pests away from your home or business in Montgomery County, Prince Georges County, Fairfax County, and DC year-round. Proactive pest control is the key to a pest-free future.

Our Protection Methods

Regular Inspections

Comprehensive property assessments to identify potential issues:

  • Quarterly property inspections
  • Early detection of pest activity
  • Identification of entry points
  • Detailed inspection reports

Barrier Treatments

Creating protective barriers around your property:

  • Perimeter treatments
  • Foundation protection
  • Entry point sealing
  • Safe, long-lasting products

Exclusion Services

Preventing pest entry through structural improvements:

  • Sealing cracks and gaps
  • Installing door sweeps
  • Repairing damaged screens
  • Improving ventilation
