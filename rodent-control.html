Health Risks of Rodent Infestations

Disease Transmission

Rodents can carry and transmit various diseases through their droppings and urine.

Allergens

Rodent dander and droppings can trigger allergies and asthma attacks.

Property Damage

Rodents can chew through wires, insulation, and structural elements.

Food Contamination

Rodents can contaminate food supplies and preparation areas.

Rodent Prevention Tips

Seal Entry Points

  • Inspect your property for gaps and holes
  • Seal cracks in foundation and walls
  • Install door sweeps and weather stripping

Maintain Cleanliness

  • Store food in sealed containers
  • Regularly clean up crumbs and spills
  • Keep garbage in sealed bins

Landscape Management

  • Trim vegetation away from buildings
  • Remove potential nesting sites
  • Keep firewood stored away from structures
