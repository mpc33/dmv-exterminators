Signs of Termite Infestation

Mud Tubes

Small tunnels made of mud on foundation walls or in crawl spaces.

Hollow Wood

Wood that sounds hollow when tapped or appears damaged.

Swarmers

Winged termites or discarded wings near windows or doors.

Frass

Termite droppings that resemble sawdust or coffee grounds.
