Frequently Asked Questions

Are the treatments safe for my family and pets?

We prioritize the safety of your family and pets by using EPA-approved products and following strict application guidelines. Our technicians are trained to apply treatments in a manner that minimizes any potential risks.

Will the pests be completely eliminated?

Our experienced technicians conduct thorough inspections to identify the source of the infestation and develop a targeted treatment plan. We are committed to completely eliminating the pests and will work with you to ensure your home is pest-free

How much will the service cost?

We strive to offer high-quality pest control services at a competitive price point within the local market. The exact cost will depend on the type of pest, the size of the infestation, and the size of your property, so we recommend contacting us for a free, no-obligation quote.

Are the treatments environmentally friendly?

We do offer eco-friendly chemical services. We understand the importance of protecting the environment and offer a range of treatment options that utilize more environmentally conscious products. Our team can discuss these options with you to find the best solution for your needs.

How quickly can you respond to my pest problem?

We understand that pest problems can be urgent and disruptive. Depending on our current schedule and the severity of the issue, we can often respond as soon as the same day or the next day. Please call us to discuss your specific situation, and we will do our best to schedule your service as quickly as possible.

Do you offer any guarantee?

Our guarantee is built on our 20 years of experience and our commitment to effective pest control. We are confident in our ability to get rid of pests, typically within 3 visits or less. To ensure long-term pest prevention, we also offer preventative treatment plans tailored to your specific needs.
