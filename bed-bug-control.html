Signs of Bed Bug Infestation

Bite Marks

Small, red, itchy welts in a line or cluster pattern on exposed skin.

Blood Stains

Tiny blood spots on sheets, pillowcases, or mattresses.

Fecal Spots

Dark, rust-colored spots on bedding, furniture, or walls.

Musty Odor

A sweet, musty smell in heavily infested areas.

Bed Bug Prevention Tips

Travel Precautions

  • Inspect hotel rooms before unpacking
  • Keep luggage on luggage racks
  • Wash clothes in hot water after travel

Home Protection

  • Use protective mattress covers
  • Regularly vacuum and clean
  • Reduce clutter in bedrooms

Secondhand Items

  • Inspect used furniture before bringing home
  • Wash secondhand clothing immediately
  • Consider professional inspection of large items
